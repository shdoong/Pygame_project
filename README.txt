Instructions:
The game is Monster Eat Cookies.
To play, use the arrow keys to control the cookie monster to eat the cookies.
However, beware cookies that are bigger than you! They will squash you.
You can only eat the cookies that are smaller than you, and each cookie you successfully devour will make you bigger so that you can eat the bigger cookies. 
The goal is to eat all the cookies!
